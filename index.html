ABOR <-> IBOR Reconciliation

Accurate, local reconciliation for operations and accounting

Compare ABOR and IBOR position statements to quantify differences in quantities and notionals. Flag material breaks before day-end and triage faster.

Common Breaks
  • Quantity mismatch between books
  • Stale or inconsistent prices
  • Derivatives multipliers / contract sizes
  • Corporate actions not applied (splits, mergers)
  • FX rounding and currency conversions
  • Duplicates or missing instruments
How It Works
  • Deterministic logic: computed in-browser; no LLM.
  • Fuzzy matching: optionally merges near-identical instrument names.
  • Grounded inputs: CSV/PDF parsing; prices, multipliers, explicit notionals.
  • Materiality: flags when |Diff Notional| / ABOR AUM >= threshold.
What You'll Need
  • ABOR & IBOR as CSV or PDF
  • Columns: instrument, qty, price; optional multiplier, notional
  • Duplicates allowed; they are aggregated
What You Get
  • Per-instrument diffs: qty & notional
  • Sorting, filters, exception highlights
  • Flags: missing price/notional, FX rounding, corporate actions
Data & Limits
  • Local-only; no uploads or storage
  • Multipliers must be provided for futures/derivatives
  • Heuristics: validate before action
